Problem statement

Gig workers — delivery riders, drivers, freelancers — have no income protection for sick days, accidents, or lost work. Existing insurance products require paperwork and upfront annual premiums that don't fit irregular gig income.

The idea

A pay-per-day micro-insurance product, deducted automatically from gig platform payouts, with claims settled within 48 hours via a simple app flow. Target market: 12M+ gig workers across India's top 20 cities.
